About

Trasher is an application to help you properly take care of our planet.

It will not only help you to identfy to which waste container your trash should be thrown in

but it will allow you to help other people and correct them if they are mistaken.

The main goal of Trasher is to show everyone how trashes should be recycled by choosing correct waste containers .
